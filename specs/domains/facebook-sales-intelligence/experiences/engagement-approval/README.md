# Experience: engagement-approval

Operator outcome: outbound engagement (comments, inbox, posts) is
approval-governed by default, deduplicated, identity-verified, and ends in
honest verified outcome states — with operator-driven recovery for anything
requiring a human.

- [business.md](business.md) — binding approval policy (default-off
  auto-execute), channel discipline, ownership predicate, success measures.
- [experience.md](experience.md) — shipped submission/verification flows and
  operator messages; planned Policy-Gate UI flagged.
